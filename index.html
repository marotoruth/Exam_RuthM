01.THE DISTRICT

The district, The future.

Museum Place is a new and uncommonly expansive part of Jing An, the upscale area in downtown Shanghai. Featuring sprawling greenery and generous open space, the Museum Place district is also distinctive for its cultural and architectural attractions,offering the public a rare experience of international art, natural history and a beautiful environment.
